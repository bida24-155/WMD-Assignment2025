Our Football Passion

At FootyShirts, we live and breathe football. Our mission is to bring fans closer to the game they love through premium quality apparel.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by lifelong football fans, FootyShirts began as a small market stall outside National Stadium. What started as a passion project quickly grew into one of the UK's most trusted football apparel retailers.

We noticed a gap in the market for high-quality, affordable football t-shirts that didn't compromise on design or comfort. Our first collection featured just 5 designs, but the response from fans was overwhelming.

Today, we work directly with clubs and designers to create exclusive collections that you won't find anywhere else. Every shirt is made with premium fabrics and undergoes rigorous quality checks before reaching our customers.

Despite our growth, we remain true to our roots - by fans, for fans. We're proud to support grassroots football initiatives across the country.

Our Values

Quality First

Fan Focused

We're fans first, business second. Every decision we make is with supporters in mind.
